About Protek Solutions

Protek Solutions is an industrial solutions provider with over 18 years of expertise in maintenance, lubricants, and reliability services. Our core business is the supply and distribution of industrial lubricants, greases, and thermic fluids.

With 15+ years of specialization in lubricants and reliability, we deliver cost‑effective, high‑performance, and sustainable solutions that reduce total cost of ownership, increase equipment reliability, and enhance operational efficiency.

Beyond product supply, we provide consultancy, technical support, and training. We work closely with clients to simplify maintenance, optimize processes, and integrate advanced technologies—while emphasizing sustainability and carbon footprint reduction.

Products

Industrial lubricants and related consumables powering reliable, efficient operations.

Industrial Oils

Hydraulic, compressor, turbine, gear, and circulating oils engineered for high performance and long drain intervals.

  • Mineral & synthetic base stocks
  • Anti‑wear and anti‑oxidation packages
  • High VI and low pour point options

Greases

Multi‑purpose, EP, high‑temp, and specialty greases for bearings, couplings, slides, and slow/oscillating systems.

  • Calcium, lithium, polyurea, and complex thickeners
  • NLGI grades and OEM specifications
  • Extreme pressure and water resistance

Thermic Fluids

High‑stability heat transfer fluids designed for efficient thermal performance and long service life.

  • High flash point and oxidative stability
  • Closed/open system compatibility
  • Food‑grade and synthetic options

Specialty & Food‑Grade

NSF H1 food‑grade lubricants and specialty fluids for clean, regulated, or extreme operating environments.

  • H1/H2 certified products
  • High‑temperature chain oils
  • Pneumatic & air line lubricants
